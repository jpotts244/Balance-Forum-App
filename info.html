About balance

balance was created to provide a supportive and positive community for those with repetitive strain injuries or anyone looking to implement more harmony and balance in their life. balance is meant to be a support group for those looking to explore a more pain-free lifestyle.

Much of the topics discussed on balance are based around integrating lifestyle change. Promoting a healthy diet and balanced posture through practicing yoga and The Alexander Technique to aid in pain relieve, joint and muscle strain, and tendon injuries.
